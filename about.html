About Me

I’ve been learning and engaging in design for years, my passion is making products with great User Experience. I have tackled difficult design challenges, mentored teams and shipped products with great UX.

I achieve results by recognizing the underlying patterns in a design that can make or break a design. I understand what can be dropped, what needs to be retained, and what needs to be developed. Finally, I know how to package design requirements for developers to make great products.
