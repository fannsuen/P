Show and Sell

An integrated mobile PoS system you can take anywhere at anytime, sell online as easily as in your physical store.

Project Intro

The SAP Anywhere Show and Sell mobile app for iPad is a point-of-sale software that can be accessed anywhere and anytime. This customizable mobile app connects to the SAP Anywhere solution and offers retail managers a virtual product showroom, in-person checkout, inventory management, and customer management right from their iPad.

Key Features:
• Showcase products with variant-based, full-screen images
• Check on the inventory across all stores
• Accept various payment methods
• Link customers to orders and keep track of their purchases
• Refund products
• Connect with retail hardware, such as a bar code scanner or a receipt printer

Time：2014.12 - 2015.9

My role in the team

This projects had two designers, the other designer mainly focus on user requirements collection and analysis. My duties encompassed research, mockup/prototype/guideline/visual design and testing.

The most challenging thing is that the whole dev team is in another city, and the project was iterating fast, so as the person who is responsible for the quality of product exprience, I wrote a very clear interaction design document and visual guide, and align with the dev team. Besides that, I paid attention to each and every version of the product before its release, make sure our user do get the best experience as we expected.

Works and Contributions

Design Principle: Like LEGO blocks, our PoS system have active panels.Different panels can combine together and build up different pages. When you trigger a new action, every pannel will slip into current page smoothly. It will not disturb current user, and make them feel the relationship between the task flow.

We see each panel as a LEGO block.

Function Panels: We take each function panel as a LEGO blocks, like Product Grid, Shopping Cart, Camera Scanner, Checkout, Numeric Keyboard, Order List, Order Detail and so on. Then we just need to combine them together based on the user's need.

Function panels

Interaction Structure: At the beginning, the structure was pretty clear and simple, we defined this structure based on major workflows of the user with PO, and all requirements must align with the structure. As the product becomes more complex, we will also update the structure, and of course the good scalability is already considered at the beginning.

Interaction Structure

User Interface Design: As a efficiency tool, the clarity and contrast of information is the highest priority, so we design the interface with the most concise UI.
