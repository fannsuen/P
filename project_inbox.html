Smart Inbox

Increasing sales productivity by bringing email, calendar, and CRM together. makes it easy to ingage with your customers and orders.

Project Intro

Inbox provide full visibility and an accurate view of the customer in SAP Anywhere, gives reps everything they need to stay on top of each and every opportunity on any device, helps get better data in how they are performing at every step of the customer lifecycle.

Inbox allows sales reps to quickly log their communications straight from their inbox, make the desktop experience more powerful with email plug-ins for Gmail and Outlook, and turn phone or tablet into a portable sales-productivity generator with the Inbox mobile app.

Time：2015.09 - 2016.02

My role in the team

I’m the only designer of this project, the scope of my duties encompassed research, user requirements collection and analysis, mockup/prototype/visual design.

Works and Contributions

Design Principle: The problem we want to solve is, we hope the reps don't have to go to front office every time after they read a e-mail. So show the correct front office information nicely is important, the full-height information pop-over window I created perfectly solve this probelm: No mattern what you click, you can always get the information in a very large and stable pop-over.

Full-height information pop-over

Smart Inbox: SAP HANA will analysis the email and highlight the data that match with front office data. Reps can reply the email fast and easy with those information displayed directly in inbox. And if everything go well, reps can create a lead or even sales orders directly.
